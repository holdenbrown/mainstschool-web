Our Teaching Philosophy

Fostering Lifelong Learners

Our goal is to model the amazement of discovery and the payoff of perseverance.

We nurture a child's natural curiosity to create a love of learning that stays with them throughout life.

This philosophy places emphasis on the students' role in the learning experience, allowing them to engage at their own level and in their own time while embracing the challenge of meeting their individual goals.

What is Project-Based Learning?

Project-based learning integrates multiple subjects and skills into meaningful, hands-on assignments.

A project is comprised of a generalized mini-lesson explaining some skill that students of all grades can understand. Students then practice with the teacher one-on-one or in similarly skilled small groups before moving on to personalized skill appropriate independent work.

This approach teaches students to process and integrate diverse information from multiple sources, developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills that prepare them for real-world challenges.
