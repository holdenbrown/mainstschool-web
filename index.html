Why Choose Main Street School?

Culture as Hidden Curriculum

Our school culture serves as a powerful hidden curriculum, teaching students values, social skills, and character development through daily interactions and community building.

Inclusive Environment

We create an inclusive environment where every student feels valued, supported, and empowered to reach their full potential regardless of background or learning style.

Diverse Learning Experiences

Students engage in art, music, hands-on activities, and outdoor play on our newly built playground, providing a well-rounded educational experience.

Hands-On Learning

Our students learn through hands-on experiences, practical applications, and real-world projects that make learning engaging and meaningful.

Personalized Curriculum

Each student receives a personalized curriculum tailored to their individual needs, learning style, and academic goals.

Preschool to 12th Grade

Serving students from preschool through 12th grade, we provide a continuous educational journey in a supportive, family-like environment.

Pursuing ISACS Accreditation

Main Street School is currently in the process of pursuing ISACS (Independent Schools Association of the Central States) accreditation. This accreditation will enable our families to access Iowa Student First Educational Savings Account (ESA) benefits.
