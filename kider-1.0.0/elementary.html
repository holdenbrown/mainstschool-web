Program Overview

Our elementary classrooms are a unique and stimulating learning experience very appropriate to our environment. It is comprised of early elementary and upper elementary with curriculum that is individualized to each student's needs.

We do not practice 'one size fits all' learning, our belief being that the needs of individual students are different, and thus, must be differently addressed. This philosophy places an emphasis on the students' role in the learning experience, allowing them to engage at their own level and in their own time.

They embrace the challenge of meeting their individual goals.

Daily Learning Experience

Morning Routine
  • Nature Start: We begin outside taking time to be in nature and wake up our bodies.
  • Morning Meeting: Group gathering to set the tone for the day.
  • Learning Centers: Students work in chosen centers with diverse materials and activities.
Independent Work
  • Learning Plans: Students choose work from their individualized learning plans.
  • Time Management: Students learn to manage their time and complete work by day's end.
  • Independence: Developing decision-making and self-directed learning skills.

Holistic Development

Creative Pursuits
  • Art Classes: Weekly art instruction to develop creativity and expression.
  • Music Classes: Weekly music education to foster appreciation and skills.
  • Friday Explorations: Afternoon adventures exploring the woods and nature.
Emotional Development
  • Group Discussion: Opportunities for peer cooperation and communication.
  • Journaling: Personal reflection to develop self-awareness.
  • Social Skills: Learning to "read" others' emotions and interact positively.
Key Benefits
Individualized Learning: Curriculum tailored to each student's needs
Self-Paced: Students engage at their own level and time
Creative Expression: Art, music, and nature exploration
Emotional Intelligence: Development of social and emotional skills
